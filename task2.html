Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. add Chocolate chips and stir well
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown

Ingredients

Ingredients Quality
flour 2 cups
sugar 2 cups
Chocolate Chip 1 cups

Enjoy your homemade cookies!
